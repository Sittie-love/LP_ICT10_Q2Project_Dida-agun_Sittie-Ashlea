About our school

O.B. Montessori Center is a private school founded by Dr. Preciosa Soliven in 1966. It blends humanitarian legacy of Operation Brotherhood with Dr. Montessori’s methods to develop self-developing, responsible citizens. It offers a complete K-12 program, offering also a progresive alternative education with multiple campuses across the Philippines.

OBMC’s mission is to nurture leaders who are self-developing, productive, and committed to serving their communities and the world. Beyond Academics, OBMC integrates community service, outdoor programs, and social responsibility into its core values.
